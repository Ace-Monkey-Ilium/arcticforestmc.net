Chat Offences

Severity 1

For all Severity 1 Chat offences (unless otherwise specified), you will be verbally warned and assigned a paper warning together. If you already have a previous warning or jail in the past month from the time of punishment for the same offence, no warning is necessary and you will be jailed. If you complain about being jailed in any way you will be banned with no warnings.
However, if there is no previous warning/jail in the past month, you are required to be warned before being jailed.
